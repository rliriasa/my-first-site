Est. 2025 · Targeting $1B AUM · Two-Partner Structure

Liria Investments is not a traditional fund. There is no large analyst team producing consensus reports, no coverage pressure, no client entertainment budget. There are two partners with deep institutional backgrounds, a growing fleet of AI agents running research around the clock, and a focus on two opportunity sets that most allocators systematically under-own.

The two partners bring complementary experience across bulge bracket equity research and hedge fund portfolio management. Both have spent careers inside the machine: prime brokerage, long/short equity, emerging markets, and direct fundamental research across Asia, Latin America, and the Gulf. They left to do the work without the constraints.

The AI layer is not a marketing story. Agents continuously monitor filings, earnings transcripts, central bank communications, and news signals across all coverage names. Humans set the thesis and make the decisions. Agents handle the work that used to require a team of six analysts.

The target is $1 billion in assets under management, built through a concentrated, high-conviction book. We have no interest in growing beyond that if it means diluting the edge.

The mandate
Pillar I: Emerging Markets
Dominant franchises in high-growth, underserved economies. LatAm, India, South Korea, Saudi Arabia, Argentina. Businesses compounding at rates impossible in developed markets, priced at discounts that reflect geography rather than quality.
Pillar II: Futurist Bets
Early-stage physical technology companies building infrastructure that will either dominate or disappear within a decade. Space launch, satellite connectivity, advanced nuclear, the lunar economy. Small positions, large asymmetry, patient capital.
