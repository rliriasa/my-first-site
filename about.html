Est. 2025 · Targeting $1B AUM · Two-Partner Structure

Liria Investments is not a traditional fund. There is no large analyst team producing consensus reports, no coverage pressure, no client entertainment budget. There are two partners who came out of the institutional world, a growing fleet of AI agents running research around the clock, and a focus on two opportunity sets that most allocators systematically under-own.

One partner trained at Morgan Stanley's Sales & Trading desk — learning how institutional capital actually moves, how large orders are structured, and how markets behave in real time under pressure. The other worked as a data scientist at Point72, one of the world's largest and most sophisticated multi-manager hedge funds — building quantitative research infrastructure and understanding how top-tier investment teams process signals and manage information at scale.

Neither arrived through the traditional analyst-to-PM ladder. That's intentional. The edge here is not seniority on a sell-side desk — it is the combination of institutional market structure knowledge, hedge fund data methodology, and the ability to build and deploy AI research agents that compress what used to take a team of six into a two-person operation running around the clock.

The target is $1 billion in assets under management, built through a concentrated, high-conviction book. We have no interest in growing beyond that if it means diluting the edge.

Investment Philosophy
"Find the best businesses in the world operating in the largest addressable markets, buy them when they are unfairly punished, and let compounding do the rest. Then use every tool available to find them faster than anyone else."
The Two Partners
Morgan Stanley S&T: trained on the Sales & Trading desk — institutional market structure, capital flows, order execution, and how Wall Street's largest desks actually operate
Point72 data science: built quantitative research infrastructure at one of the world's most sophisticated multi-manager platforms — signal research, systematic data pipelines, and front-row exposure to how elite hedge funds process information
AI-native from day one: the fund was designed around AI agents, not retrofitted. Research, monitoring, and modelling are agent-first — which is why two people can cover ten names with the depth of a full team
The AI Agent Layer
Always-on monitoring: agents track filings, transcripts, macro releases, short interest changes, and news across all coverage names 24 hours a day
Signal extraction: models parse earnings calls for tone shifts, capex guidance changes, and language patterns that tend to precede estimate revisions before consensus catches up
Model automation: financial models update automatically on new data releases, scenarios refresh in real time, sensitivity tables rebuild without manual intervention
Lean by design: the research output of a 6-person team, delivered by 2 humans and a growing agent fleet, keeping the cost base lean and the alignment clean

The mandate
Pillar I: Emerging Markets
Dominant franchises in high-growth, underserved economies. LatAm, India, South Korea, Saudi Arabia, Argentina. Businesses compounding at rates impossible in developed markets, priced at discounts that reflect geography rather than quality.
Pillar II: Futurist Bets
Early-stage physical technology companies building infrastructure that will either dominate or disappear within a decade. Space launch, satellite connectivity, advanced nuclear, the lunar economy. Small positions, large asymmetry, patient capital.
Target AUM
$1 billion. Enough to be a serious allocator at every name in the book. Small enough to stay nimble and concentrated. We have no interest in growing beyond that if it means diluting the edge.
Time Horizon
3 to 5 years minimum on core positions. We are not trading. We are buying businesses we want to own through cycles, with the patience that most institutions claim to have but rarely demonstrate.
Structure

What we are not
A quant fund. A macro fund. A multi-strategy platform. A fund of funds. We do one thing: find the best asymmetric long opportunities on the planet in two defined categories, and own them with conviction.
