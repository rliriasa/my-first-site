AI Infrastructure
AI Research Agent Architect

Futurist Bets
Generalist — Futurist Bets Analyst
Full-time Remote

You will cover the Futurist Bets sleeve: space launch, satellite connectivity, advanced nuclear, lunar economy, and adjacent deep-tech infrastructure plays. The companies in this bucket are early-stage, illiquid, and binary — deep technical literacy matters as much as financial modelling. You'll build scenario trees, track milestones, and help size positions where the data is thin and the upside is generational.

  • Background in institutional equity research, venture capital, or hedge fund investing with specific coverage of space, energy, or advanced hardware sectors
  • Technical literacy: comfortable reading engineering filings, launch manifests, regulatory submissions, and manufacturing cost curves
  • Experience using AI agents to track non-traditional signals — government contract databases, launch schedules, patent filings, academic pre-prints
  • Frameworks background (LLM tooling, agent orchestration) or strong willingness to develop it on the job
  • Exceptional writing: these are difficult ideas to size; your job is to make the case clearly and quantitatively under uncertainty
  • Curiosity that borders on obsessive — this sleeve only works if the analyst genuinely believes these technologies will define the next two decades
